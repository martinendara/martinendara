## Featured Project

### [Lotus — AI Citation Intelligence Engine](https://github.com/martinendara/agentic-seo-lotus)

Production-grade GEO platform deployed on Google Cloud Run. Monitors AI crawler activity,
generates structured defense artifacts, runs competitive intelligence pipelines, and quantifies
revenue at risk from AI zero-click erosion.

**Core capabilities:**
- **Citation monitoring** — tracks how LLMs represent and cite a brand across AI search engines
- **Competitive intelligence** — discovers and analyzes competitor GEO strategies via scraping + Gemini
- **Artifact generation** — produces `llms.txt`, JSON-LD structured data, and semantic context layers
- **Revenue at risk model** — CFO-grade bleed quantification with GSC integration and confidence scoring
- **Quick wins engine** — prioritized GEO action queue with state tracking per client
- **White Label Agency mode** — agencies manage up to 5 clients under their own brand, isolated by `agency_id`
- **MCP Server** — exposes `analyze_geo`, `get_quick_wins`, and `get_competitor_actions` as tools
  for AI agents (Claude, Cursor, etc.) to interact with Lotus programmatically

`FastAPI` `Gemini API` `Playwright` `Firebase` `Docker` `Cloud Run` `MCP` `GEO`
